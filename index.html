Welcome to Procrast-A-Nation!


If you, like me, often feel compelled to take a break from all the stress and worry caused by the modern-day pressures associated with work and studies – then look no further than Procrast-A-Nation! A site where the urge to procrastinate isn’t condemned but encouraged!


“Procrastination is the action of delaying or postponing something. The word has origin from the Latin procrastinatus, which itself evolved from the prefix pro-, meaning "forward," and crastinus, meaning "of tomorrow." - Wikipedia


After all, to procrastinate is part of the human experience. Maybe your brain is just telling you to take a break and have a little bit of fun in amongst the hard work (and sometimes boredom) of your regular life. Which is why on this webpage you can take a moment away from life to play some quick, yet entertaining games, which may also help to improve your memory/hand-eye co-ordination etc! It’s all good stuff!


So welcome to the site and enjoy your time away from the ‘real’ world as you indulge in some well-earned procrastination!
