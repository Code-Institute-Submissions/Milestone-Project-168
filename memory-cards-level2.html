Memory Card Game


The objective is to locate the pairs of cards, a test of your memory. When you turn over two cards that do not match, those cards are turned face down again. To win the game you need to match each pair of cards. The trick is to remember which cards are where.

Level 2 - Wonders of the World!

There are 8 pairs of iconic travel desinations to match - to win the game you need to match all 8 pairs in 60 seconds! See how fast you can do it and how many moves it takes you! Plus why not see how many you've been to or wish to visit.


The timer will start when you click on the first card - good luck!
